Level 1 — First Words

D is for Dog

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
D

D can say /d/, like dog.

dog
duck
dad
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
dogd-o-g
duckd-u-c-k
dadd-a-d
dend-e-n
dotd-o-t
dishd-i-s-h
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a dog.

The dog saw a duck.

Dad had a dish.

A dot was on the dish.

Cat went in the den.

Dog did too.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned D.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
