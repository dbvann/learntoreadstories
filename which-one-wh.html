Which One?

Read wh words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound Team2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound Team
wh

w and h work together in words like which.

Step 2 · Read a Few Words
whichwh-i-ch
whitewh-ite
wheelwh-ee-l
whilewh-ile
Step 3 · Tiny Story

The girl had two shells.

One was white.

One had a wheel shape.

"Which one?" said the girl.

Beach Cat looked for a while.

He tapped the white one.

"That one," said the girl.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read wh words like which, white, wheel, and while.

You used wh to read a choice story.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
