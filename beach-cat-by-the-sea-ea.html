Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Beach Cat by the Sea

Read one gentle story with ea. The letters ea can say long e, like in sea.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ea

ea can say long e, like sea.

sea
beach
read
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
beachb-ea-ch
seas-ea
readr-ea-d
eachea-ch
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat is on the beach.

He can see the sea.

A book is by the sand.

Beach Cat can read each word.

The sea is calm.

Beach Cat likes the beach.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🏖️

Now you read words with ea.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
