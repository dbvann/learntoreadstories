Level 2 — Short Vowel Stories

Sam Has a Map

Read three short-a words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
A

A can say /ă/, like map.

🗺️map
👦Sam
✋has
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
SamS-a-m
hash-a-s
mapm-a-p
ata-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Sam has a map.

Sam sat.

Sam had a map.

Cat sat at the map.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read Sam, has, and map.

That’s real reading.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
