Step 3 · Tiny Story

The girl had a blue ball.

The blue ball rolled across the sand.

Beach Cat saw the ball.

Beach Cat gave a blink.

The blue ball rolled back.

"Good cat," said the girl.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read bl words like blue and blink.

That blend has two sounds sliding together.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
