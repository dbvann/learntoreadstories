Level 1 — First Words

E is for Egg

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
E

E can say /ɛ/, like egg.

egg
elephant
end
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
egge-g-g
elephante-l-e-p-h-a-n-t
ende-n-d
elfe-l-f
elbowe-l-b-o-w
emptye-m-p-t-y
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw an egg.

An elf sat by the egg.

An elephant came to the end.

The cup was empty.

Cat bumped an elbow.

Everyone said, ‘E!’

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned E.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
