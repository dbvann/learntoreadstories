Level 1 — First Words

H is for Hat

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
H

H can say /h/, like hat.

hat
hen
hop
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
hath-a-t
henh-e-n
hoph-o-p
househ-o-u-s-e
hillh-i-l-l
handh-a-n-d
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat had a hat.

A hen sat by the house.

Cat went up the hill.

The hen did a hop.

A hand held the hat.

Hi, hen!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned H.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
