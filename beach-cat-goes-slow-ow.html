Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Beach Cat Goes Slow

Read one gentle story with ow. In this story, ow says long o, like in slow.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ow

ow can say long o, like slow.

slow
low
grow
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
slowsl-ow
lowl-ow
snowsn-ow
growgr-ow
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat goes slow.

The sun is low.

A little snow is on the sand.

Beach Cat sees a low flower.

“Grow, little flower,” says Beach Cat.

Beach Cat goes slow by the shore.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🐾

Now you read words with ow.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
