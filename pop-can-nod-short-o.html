Pop Can Nod

Read short-o words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
O

O can say /ŏ/, like nod.

nodn-o-d

Step 3 · Tiny Story

Pop had a pot.

The pot sat on a log.

Pop can nod.

Pop nods at the pot.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read Pop, nod, and pot.

That’s real reading.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
