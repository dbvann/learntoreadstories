Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Beach Cat Gets Mail

Read one gentle story with ai. The letters ai can say long a, like in mail.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ai

ai can say long a, like mail.

mail
tail
rain
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
mailm-ai-l
tailt-ai-l
waitw-ai-t
rainr-ai-n
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Rain taps the sand.

Beach Cat sees mail.

The mail is by a pail.

Beach Cat taps his tail.

He will wait.

The mail is safe.

Step 4 · Celebrate
💌

Now you read more words with ai.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
