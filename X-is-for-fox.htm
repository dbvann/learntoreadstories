Level 1 — First Words

X is for Fox

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
X

X can say /ks/ at the end, like fox.

box
fox
six
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
boxb-o-x
foxf-o-x
sixs-i-x
oxo-x
mixedm-i-x-e-d
fixf-i-x
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a fox.

The fox had a box.

Six shells sat in the box.

An ox bumped the box.

The shells got mixed.

Cat helped fix the box.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned X.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
