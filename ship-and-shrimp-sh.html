Ship and Shrimp

Read sh words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound Team2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound Team
sh

s and h say /sh/, like ship.

🚢ship
🦐shrimp
🤫Shhh
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
shipsh-i-p
shoresh-ore
shrimpsh-r-i-m-p
Shhh!sh sound
Step 3 · Tiny Story

A ship was by the shore.

Beach Cat saw the ship.

A shrimp was on the ship.

The shrimp said, “Shhh!”

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read sh words like ship and shrimp.

That’s a new sound team.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
