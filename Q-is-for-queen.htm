Level 1 — First Words

Q is for Queen

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
Q

Q and U can say qu, like queen.

queen
quilt
quack
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
queenq-u-e-e-n
quiltq-u-i-l-t
quackq-u-a-c-k
quickq-u-i-c-k
quietq-u-i-e-t
quarterq-u-a-r-t-e-r
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a queen.

The queen had a quilt.

A duck said, quack.

Cat was quick.

Then Cat was quiet.

A quarter sat on the quilt.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned Q.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
