Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Grow, Little Flower

Read one gentle story with ow. In this story, ow says long o, like in grow.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ow

ow can say long o, like grow.

grow
low
slow
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
growgr-ow
lowl-ow
slowsl-ow
yellowyell-ow
Step 3 · Tiny Story

A little flower is low.

It is yellow.

Beach Cat sits slow.

“Grow, little flower,” he says.

The sun is low.

The flower can grow.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🌱

Now you read words with ow.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
