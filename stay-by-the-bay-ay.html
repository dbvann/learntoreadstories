Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Stay by the Bay

Read one gentle story with ay. The letters ay can say long a, like in stay.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ay

ay can say long a, like stay.

stay
bay
day
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
stayst-ay
bayb-ay
dayd-ay
playpl-ay
Step 3 · Tiny Story

It is a calm day.

Beach Cat is by the bay.

He can stay.

He can play.

The bay is bright.

Beach Cat likes this day.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🌊

Now you read words with ay.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
