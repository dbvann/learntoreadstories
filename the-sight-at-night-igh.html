The Sight at Night

Read one gentle story with igh. The letters igh say long i, like in sight.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
igh

igh can say long i, like sight.

night

Step 3 · Tiny Story

It is night.

Beach Cat sees a light.

The light is bright.

It is high.

What a sight!

Beach Cat likes the night.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

That’s a win.
