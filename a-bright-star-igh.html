Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

A Bright Star

Read one gentle story with igh. The letters igh say long i, like in bright.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
igh

igh can say long i, like bright.

bright
light
night
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
brightbr-igh-t
lightl-igh-t
highh-igh
nightn-igh-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

It is night.

Beach Cat sees a star.

The star is bright.

The star is high.

Beach Cat sits in the light.

What a bright night!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you read words with igh.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
