Level 1 — First Words

R is for Rat

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
R

R can say r, like rat.

rat
red
rug
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
ratr-a-t
redr-e-d
rugr-u-g
runr-u-n
rockr-o-c-k
rainr-a-i-n
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a rat.

The rat ran by a rock.

A red rug sat in the rain.

Cat ran too.

The rain went drip.

Run, rat, run!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned R.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
