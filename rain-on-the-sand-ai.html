Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Rain on the Sand

Read one gentle story with ai. The letters ai can say long a, like in rain.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ai

ai can say long a, like rain.

rain
pail
sail
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
rainr-ai-n
pailp-ai-l
waitw-ai-t
sails-ai-l
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Rain falls on the sand.

Beach Cat has a pail.

The pail sits in the rain.

Beach Cat will wait.

A little sail is in the pail.

Rain can be fun.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🌧️

Now you read words with ai.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
