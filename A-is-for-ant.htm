A is for Ant

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
A

A can say /ă/ like ant.

ant
apple
alligator
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
anta-n-t
catc-a-t
sats-a-t
ata-t

alligatora-l-l-i-g-a-t-o-r
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat sat.

Cat sat at an apple.

An ant ran.

Cat saw the ant.

Cat saw an alligator.

“Ah!” said Cat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned A.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
