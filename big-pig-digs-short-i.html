Level 2 — Short Vowel Stories

Big Pig Digs

Read short-i words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
I

I can say /ĭ/, like pig.

🐷pig
⭐big
🕳️pit
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
bigb-i-g
pigp-i-g
digd-i-g
pitp-i-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

The big pig can dig.

The pig digs a pit.

The pit is big.

The pig sits by it.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read big, pig, and dig.

That’s real reading.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
