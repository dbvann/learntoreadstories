Level 1 — First Words

J is for Jam

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
J

J can say /dʒ/, like jam.

jam
jet
jug
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
jamj-a-m
jetj-e-t
jugj-u-g
jumpj-u-m-p
jellyfishj-e-l-l-y-f-i-s-h
jarj-a-r
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a jar.

The jar had jam.

A jug sat by the jar.

Cat saw a jet in the sky.

A jellyfish went jump?

No, said Cat!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned J.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
