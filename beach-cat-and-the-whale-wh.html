Beach Cat and the Whale

Read wh words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound Team2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound Team
wh

w and h work together in words like whale.

🐋whale
👃whiff
🐱whisker
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
whalewh-ale
whiffwh-i-ff
whitewh-ite
whiskerwh-i-s-k-er
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat sat by the sea.

A whale went by.

The whale made a big splash.

Beach Cat got a whiff of fish.

His white whisker got wet.

“That whale is big,” said Cat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read wh words like whale, whiff, white, and whisker.

You read wh in beach words and story words.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
