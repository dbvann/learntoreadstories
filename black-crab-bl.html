Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat saw a black crab.

The crab sat by a block.

Beach Cat blinked.

The crab blinked back.

Beach Cat sat still.

The black crab ran away.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read bl words like black, blink, and block.

You can hear both sounds in a blend.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
