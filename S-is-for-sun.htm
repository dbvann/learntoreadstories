Level 1 — First Words

S is for Sun

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
S

S can say s, like sun.

sun
sand
sock
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
suns-u-n
sands-a-n-d
socks-o-c-k
seals-e-a-l
sits-i-t
shells-h-e-l-l
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat sat in the sun.

Cat sat on the sand.

A seal had a sock.

Cat saw a shell.

Cat can sit.

So can the seal.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned S.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
