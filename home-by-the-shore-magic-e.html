Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Home by the Shore

Read one gentle story with Magic E. A quiet e can help the vowel say its name, like in home.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
o_e

In home, the quiet e helps o say its name.

home
bone
rope
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
homeh-o-me
boneb-o-ne
roper-o-pe
wavew-a-ve
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat is by the shore.

He sees a wave.

A rope is on the sand.

A bone is by the rope.

Beach Cat can go home.

Home is by the shore.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🏠

Now you read Magic E words like home.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
