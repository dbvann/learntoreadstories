Level 1 — First Words

T is for Turtle

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
T

T can say t, like turtle.

top
ten
tent
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
topt-o-p
tent-e-n
tentt-e-n-t
turtlet-u-r-t-l-e
tapt-a-p
tailt-a-i-l
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a turtle.

The turtle had a top.

Cat gave a tap.

The top went ten times.

Cat saw a tent.

Turtle wagged a tail.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned T.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
