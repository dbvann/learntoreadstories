Whisper by the Waves

Read wh words. Then read one tiny story.

Step 2 · Read a Few Words
whisperwh-i-s-p-er

whalewh-ale
Step 3 · Tiny Story

The waves went whish, whish.

Beach Cat heard a whisper.

"Why are you here?" said Cat.

The whisper came again.

A little whale was by the rocks.

Beach Cat sat with the whale.

The waves went whish, whish.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐
