igh Stories

The letters igh can say long i, like in night. Read simple stories with words like light, bright, night, sight, and high.

🌙

Words: night, light, bright, sight. A gentle igh story about a soft light by the sea.

Parent Tip: Point to igh and say, “These three letters work together to say long i.” Let your child circle or tap one igh word before reading each story.
