Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Feet in the Sand

Read one gentle story with ee. The letters ee can say long e, like in feet.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ee

ee can say long e, like feet.

feet
seed
deep
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
feetf-ee-t
sees-ee
seeds-ee-d
deepd-ee-p
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat has feet in the sand.

He can see a seed.

The seed is not deep.

Beach Cat taps the sand.

His feet make a print.

Beach Cat can see it.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🐾

Now you read words with ee.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
