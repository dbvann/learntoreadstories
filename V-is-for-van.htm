Level 1 — First Words

V is for Van

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
V

V can say v, like van.

van
vest
vine
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
vanv-a-n
vestv-e-s-t
vinev-i-n-e
vasev-a-s-e
visorv-i-s-o-r
volleyballv-o-l-l-e-y-b-a-l-l
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a van.

A wave brought a vest.

A vine came by.

Cat saw a vase.

A visor rode the wave.

Then came a volleyball.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned V.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
