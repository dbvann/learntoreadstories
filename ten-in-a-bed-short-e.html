Level 2 — Short Vowel Stories

Ten in a Bed

Read short-e words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
E

E can say /ĕ/, like bed.

🔟ten
🛏️bed
🔴red
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
tent-e-n
bedb-e-d
redr-e-d
petp-e-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Ten pets sat.

Ten pets sat on a bed.

The bed was red.

Ben fed the pets.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read ten, bed, and red.

That’s real reading.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
