Level 1 — First Words

K is for Kite

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
K

K can say k, like kite.

kite
kid
king
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
kitek-i-t-e
kidk-i-d
kingk-i-n-g
keyk-e-y
kittenk-i-t-t-e-n
kickk-i-c-k
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a kite.

A kid had the kite.

A kitten saw a key.

The king gave a kick.

The kite went up.

Kitten ran to Cat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned K.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
