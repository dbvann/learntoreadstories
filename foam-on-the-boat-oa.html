Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Foam on the Boat

Read one playful story with oa. The letters oa say long o, like in boat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
oa

oa can say long o, like boat.

foam
boat
float
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
foamf-oa-m
boatb-oa-t
floatfl-oa-t
coastc-oa-st
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Foam is on the boat.

The boat can float.

Beach Cat is by the coast.

Beach Cat sees foam.

The foam is on the boat.

The boat floats by the coast.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🫧

Now you read a story with oa.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
