Level 1 — First Words

P is for Pig

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
P

P can say /p/, like pig.

pig
pan
pot
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
pigp-i-g
panp-a-n
potp-o-t
pupp-u-p
pearp-e-a-r
pondp-o-n-d
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a pig.

The pig had a pan.

A pot sat by a pond.

A pup saw a pear.

Cat patted the sand.

Pop! went the pot.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned P.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
