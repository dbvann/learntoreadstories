Step 3 · Tiny Story

The girl had a cloth.

She made a little tent.

She clipped the cloth to an umbrella.

The wind came in.

The cloth began to lift.

The clip held the cloth.

Beach Cat sat inside the tent.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read cl words like cloth, clip, and clipped.

You used a blend in a cozy beach story.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
