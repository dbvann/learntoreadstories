Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Beach Cat on a Boat

Read one gentle story with oa. The letters oa say long o, like in boat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
oa

oa can say long o, like boat.

boat
coast
float
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
boatb-oa-t
coastc-oa-st
floatfl-oa-t
soaks-oa-k
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat sees a boat.

The boat is by the coast.

Can Beach Cat float?

Yes. The boat can float.

A wave can soak the boat.

Beach Cat is safe on the boat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⛵

Now you read words with oa.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
