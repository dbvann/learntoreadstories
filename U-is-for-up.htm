Level 1 — First Words

U is for Up

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
U

U can say short u, like up.

up
umbrella
under
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
upu-p
umbrellau-m-b-r-e-l-l-a
underu-n-d-e-r
uncleu-n-c-l-e
bugb-u-g
cupc-u-p
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat looked up.

A bug was under a cup.

Uncle had an umbrella.

Cat went under it.

The cup went up.

Up, up, up!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned U.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
