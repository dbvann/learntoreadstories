Level 1 — First Words

G is for Goat

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
G

G can say /g/, like goat.

goat
gum
gate
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
goatg-o-a-t
gumg-u-m
gateg-a-t-e
gooseg-o-o-s-e
giftg-i-f-t
grassg-r-a-s-s
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a goat.

The goat stood by a gate.

A goose sat in the grass.

Cat saw a gift.

The gift had gum.

Good, said Cat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned G.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
