Level 2 — Short Vowel Stories

Octopus Has a Rock

Read short-o words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
O

O can say /ŏ/, like octopus.

🐙octopus
📦box
🪨rock
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
octopuso-c-t-o-p-u-s
gotg-o-t
boxb-o-x
rockr-o-c-k
Step 3 · Tiny Story

An octopus got a box.

Cat saw the box.

The octopus put a rock in the box.

The octopus sat by Cat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read octopus, box, and rock.

That’s real reading.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
