Level 2 — Short Vowel Stories

Bug in a Cup

Read short-u words. Then read one tiny story.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
U

U can say the sound in bug.

bug
cup
up
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
bugb-u-g
cupc-u-p
upu-p
outo-u-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

A bug got in a cup.

Cat saw the bug.

The bug went up, up, up.

But the bug could not get out.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read bug, cup, and up.

That’s real reading.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
