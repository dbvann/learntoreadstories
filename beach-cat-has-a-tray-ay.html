Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Beach Cat Has a Tray

Read one gentle story with ay. The letters ay can say long a, like in tray.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ay

ay can say long a, like tray.

day
stay
Step 2 · Read a Few Words

Step 3 · Tiny Story

Beach Cat has a tray.

The tray is on the sand.

It is a sunny day.

Beach Cat finds shells to save.

He puts them on the tray.

The tray shines by the bay.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🐾

Now you read words with ay.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
