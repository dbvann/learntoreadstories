Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

Read by the Sea

Read one gentle story with ea. The letters ea can say long e, like in read.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
ea

ea can say long e, like read.

read
sea
teach
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
readr-ea-d
seas-ea
beachb-ea-ch
teacht-ea-ch
Step 3 · Tiny Story

A child can read by the sea.

Beach Cat sits on the beach.

The child can teach a word.

Beach Cat can see the word.

The sea is quiet.

They read by the sea.

Step 4 · Celebrate
📖

Now you read words with ea.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
