Level 1 — First Words

O is for Octopus

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
O

O can say /ɑ/, like octopus.

octopus
ox
on
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
octopuso-c-t-o-p-u-s
oxo-x
ono-n
offo-f-f
ottero-t-t-e-r
oliveo-l-i-v-e
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw an octopus.

An otter sat on a rock.

An ox came by.

An olive fell off.

Cat put it on the sand.

Odd, said Cat.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned O.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
