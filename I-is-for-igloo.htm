Level 1 — First Words

I is for Igloo

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
I

I can say short i, like igloo.

igloo
insect
inch
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
iglooi-g-l-o-o
insecti-n-s-e-c-t
inchi-n-c-h
inki-n-k
illi-l-l
itchi-t-c-h
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw an igloo.

An insect sat on it.

The insect moved one inch.

Cat saw ink.

Cat had an itch.

Cat was not ill.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned I.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
