Level 1 — First Words

N is for Nest

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
N

N can say n, like nest.

net
nest
nap
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
netn-e-t
nestn-e-s-t
napn-a-p
nosen-o-s-e
nutn-u-t
nightn-i-g-h-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a nest.

A nut sat in the nest.

Cat had a nap.

A net was by Cat.

Cat touched his nose.

Night came soon.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned N.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
