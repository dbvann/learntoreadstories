Level 1 — First Words

L is for Leaf

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound2. Read the Words3. Read the Story4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
L

L can say /l/, like leaf.

log
leaf
lamp
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
logl-o-g
leafl-e-a-f
lampl-a-m-p
lionl-i-o-n
legl-e-g
lakel-a-k-e
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat sat on a log.

A leaf fell by Cat.

Cat saw a lamp.

A lion sat by the lake.

Cat lifted one leg.

Look, a leaf!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned L.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
