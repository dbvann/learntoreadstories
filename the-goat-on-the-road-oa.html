Level 4 — Long Vowels & Sneaky Letters

The Goat on the Road

Read one gentle story with oa. The letters oa say long o, like in goat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
oa

oa can say long o, like goat.

goat
road
roam
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
goatg-oa-t
roadr-oa-d
roamr-oa-m
boatb-oa-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

A goat is on the road.

The road is by the coast.

The goat can roam.

Beach Cat sees the goat.

The goat looks at a boat.

The boat floats by the road.

Step 4 · Celebrate
🐐

Now you read more words with oa.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
