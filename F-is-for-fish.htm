Level 1 — First Words

F is for Fish

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
F

F can say f, like fish.

fan
fish
fox
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
fanf-a-n
fishf-i-s-h
foxf-o-x
frogf-r-o-g
featherf-e-a-t-h-e-r
feetf-e-e-t
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a fish.

A frog sat by the fish.

A fox had a feather.

The fan went fast.

Cat felt it on his feet.

Fun for Cat!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned F.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
