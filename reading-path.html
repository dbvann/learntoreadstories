The Reading Path

Six gentle levels that take your child from first sounds to real story reading — one short lesson at a time.

One sound. A few words. One tiny story. Ten minutes a night.

Start where your child is now.

Each level builds on the one before it. You do not have to rush. Begin with the first level that feels comfortable, then move forward when the stories feel easy.

The path is simple on purpose.

Every level keeps the same comforting rhythm: warm up with one sound, try a few words, meet those words in a tiny story, then stop before it feels like homework.
