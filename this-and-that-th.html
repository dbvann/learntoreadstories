Step 3 · Tiny Story

This is Beach Cat.

That is a shell.

The shell is on the sand.

Beach Cat sees the shell.

Then Beach Cat sits by it.

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

You read th words like this, that, the, and then.

That’s a new sound team.

If it still feels fun, read the story one more time.
