Level 1 — First Words

Z is for Zebra

Say one sound. Read a few words. Read one tiny story with Cat.

1. Say the Sound 2. Read the Words 3. Read the Story 4. Celebrate
Step 1 · Say the Sound
Z

Z can say z, like zebra.

zip
zoo
zebra
Step 2 · Read a Few Words
zipz-i-p
zooz-o-o
zebraz-e-b-r-a
zeroz-e-r-o
zigzagz-i-g-z-a-g
zipperz-i-p-p-e-r
Step 3 · Tiny Story

Cat saw a zebra.

The zebra had a zipper.

Zip, zip, zip.

A line went zigzag.

Cat counted zero bugs.

Off to the zoo!

Step 4 · Celebrate
⭐

Now you learned Z.

That’s a win.

If it still feels fun, read the story again.
